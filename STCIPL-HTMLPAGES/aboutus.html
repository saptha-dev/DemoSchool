ABOUT US

STCIPL is global and domestic training, technical skills development, global consulting services, Data management and Analytical Services Company. STCIPL is organizing best training solutions with emerging technical and best expertise teaching methods and planning to develop digitalization of training using different software applications across different organizations. Now it is starting into new regions globally with new dimension of education training services with new learning methods and global standards of training system.

We are developing best practicing methods of new way of learning system for different sectors or industries for global opportunities for learners. We are working to provide best concept learning for any category of subjects with best interaction between learner and organization at one place for domestic and global customers or industries across the region with high standards of education system, best technology implementations for practicing learners and education services.

PHILOSOPHY

VISION

Our Vision is that we are committed to provide customized and standardized training solutions and services. We wish to become the most admired and inspirational Company amongst international institutes and corporates by creating best intellectual training standards and learning practices to be a role model. We set up the best value policies and practices for training professionals by creating Professional work environment and by our focus on continuous learning and growth.

We believe in giving top learning exposure for learners with best curriculum, regular one to one practicing and training on every learning subjects and case studies.


MISSION

STCIPL’s mission is to provide best curricular, training research system with academic excellence, diverse learning styles, best integration practices to ensure that learners to build leadership skills, values of integrity and distinguished management principles for connecting with society and driving into successful careers.

Our ultimate goal is to make STCIPL as leading best tutoring, mentoring and motivational learning centre nationally and internationally to provide education for learners to use the knowledge and skills necessary for professional management and achieving their goals.
