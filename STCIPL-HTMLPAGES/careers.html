Careers

We focus on giving our people the opportunity to grow professionally, advancing their skills and careers. On recognizing and rewarding them for their efforts and achievements. But, perhaps the most important is an opportunity to make a difference

STCIPL INSTITUTE promotes a culture of continuous learning, team collaboration and individual growth. By bringing your expertise to teams with diverse talents and backgrounds, you'll gain new insight and experience each day, while contributing to a learning environment. We help to grow personally and professionally by fostering professional development, supporting opportunities to stretch skill sets, and encouraging the exploration of various career options Make a Difference: STCIPL INSTITUTE combines the stability of a ASSOC-COMPANY with a track record of success with the entrepreneurial atmosphere of a small ASSOC-COMPANY with an infrastructure of a world class organization.

We value initiative and the sharing of ideas and expertise at all levels of the organization. Right from the start, we encourage and expect each member of our team to make a significant contribution to the success of our clients. Be Recognized and Rewarded: At STCIPL INSTITUTE, we understand that the efforts and achievements of our people come from different sectors. Our incentive programs are designed to recognize past performance as well as to motivate continuing career development.

We recognize and reward individual contributions, team accomplishments, the mastery of new skills, and the mentoring of team members. We expect our people to take ownership for delivering excellent results, and to share in the rewards of success.
