  1. Programs

Description CREATING HIGHLY SKILLED PROFESSIONSALS THROUGH SDMP

Statistical Data and information management is the development of policies and systems that protect and deliver the information to organization operations.

Data Management is crucial for performance of organizations. When organization is having poor data management resources it leads to financial losses, missed opportunities, low productivity and poor reputation. Sometimes the information collected is inaccurate, inaccessible and incomplete then data management team should review the risks that need to protect against these problems

SDMP helps people to build skills for leveraging new opportunities and creating exceptional result in research to the organization. SDMP through STCIPL offers management training programs for every level of peoples from experienced working people to new learners.

A SDMP program can provide statistical research methods training with a fresh perspective on broader issues and help them to identify changing markets. In this SDMP everyone focus on sharpening skills related to challenges in research project management by working on real challenges as practice in the STCIPL programs

SDMP refines and leverage skills and gives new knowledge for seeking employees and new learners particularly in data management for different industrial requirements. STCIPL provide continuous learning for staff in any corporates, governments and research industries through different training programs. For better practicing in Data management and statistical analytics consider STCIPL SDMP that include building research strategy, research funding management, study programs management, analytics management.

THROUGH SDMP YOU CAN HAVE BEST KNOWLEDGE TO

•    Increase your ability to influence management decisions and study projects planning and implementation
•    Develop strategic plans for research program development
•    Exercise better funding management
•    Improve your statistical data management skills and learning for resources
•    Understanding key concepts on integrating new technology into the research organization
    Strategic and operational planning for research in health services, market research services. Training

Assessment

Step-1: Go to Assessment section in registered course program

Step-2: Register for Assessment category and its subjects on STCIPL website first with your personal information.

Step-3: Select your Assessment ASSOC-COMPANY and its location information.

Step-4: Book for the Assessment Real Test of your category program and its subject.

Step-5: Get booking reference number from your email.

Step-6: Attend the Assessment Real Test as per your booking slot with your booking information.

Step-7: Complete the Assessment Real Test at the Assessment ASSOC-COMPANY location.

Step-8: Get the result into your Assessment account after valuation completed by the STCIPL.
